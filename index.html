HPE DSI Courses

HPE DSI awards digital Badges to students who complete and pass each of our courses. These courses do not affect students' University GPA or Grade Point. We recommend you follow the HPE DSI course roadmap when picking courses.

HPE DSI courses are free for active University of Houston System (UHS) students, staff or faculty and $250/course for non-UH individuals.

How It Works

Each program begins with 101 Intro to HPC, a prerequisite for all HPE DSI courses, followed by a choice of scripting languages (R, Python, Julia) or C++ for scientific programming. Students can then advance to specialized courses like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, GPU Programming, or Bioinformatics. Mandatory attendance of at least 12 hours of synchronous instruction is required for pass/fail grades and badges.
